Our Products

All our products are cruelty-free, it’s a category containing
all cosmetics that have not been tested on animals. Each
cosmetic is subject to strict control by independent
inspection bodies chosen by the Coalition for Consumer
Information in Cosmetics (CCIC), which is an NGO
founded by 8 international animal protection groups such
as: American Anti-Vivisection Society, Animal Alliance of
Canada, Doris Day Animal League, etc.
